Budget Book Scanning

Budget Book Scanning offers an easy, low cost alternative for your book conversion and digitization needs.

Our company is here to help you convert your library to various e-formats, therefore easing up storage space, providing enhanced search functionality, lightens your transport load, and allowing you to access all your favorite titles on any electronic device of your choice. “Budget” Book Scanning, a subsidiary of Bound Book Scanning Inc. which has been providing various other book scanning services since 2010, is dedicated solely to providing destructive scanning services, with a simple order process, at a cut-rate
low price of $5 per book of any size.

  • How It Works

  • Count your books

    Obtain an accurate count of the titles to be scanned. All sizes are ok, page count does NOT matter. Minimum order is 10 books. (If you have less than 10 books to scan, please visit boundbookscanning.com). The base fee is
    $5 per book. Page size or page count doesn't matter.

  • Fill the form

    Fill out our order form and specify your output requirements (here).

  • Ship Your Books

    Carefully package your books in a sturdy box, ensuring that any extra space is filled with packing material and ship the boxes to our warehouse. Upon receipt, we will dissemble the books and scan them.

  • We scan, You Relax

    We will run our high-quality OCR (Optical Character Recognition) software on each book, to recognize all text with 99% accuracy so you have the ability to search any word appearing in your books.

  • Review the product

    Within one week of receipt, we will email you a link to a Dropbox folder containing your files that you can easily download to your PC or any other device
